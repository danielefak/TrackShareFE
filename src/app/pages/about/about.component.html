What is TrackShare?

TrackShare is a personal finance management tool that helps you track income, expenses, and balances across multiple accounts. Share accounts with friends and family to manage shared finances together.

Shared Finance

Connect with friends by sharing accounts. Each shared account is mirrored across both users, keeping balances synchronized. Get notified when friends modify shared accounts.

Insights

Visualize your spending patterns with charts and summaries. Categorize transactions, track monthly trends, and stay on top of your financial goals.
